Meet the founder

Compassion born from lived experience.

Sheri Blow understands the vulnerability of hair loss firsthand. Her ministry-minded mission is to create a brave, restorative space where every client is seen, heard, and celebrated.

Sheri's journey

When autoimmune hair loss reshaped Sheri's life, she realized how few places honored both the practical and emotional needs of women navigating similar diagnoses. Island Style Wigs grew from that longing—a studio where expertise and empathy meet.

With years of advanced education in color theory, cap construction, adhesive science, and custom styling, Sheri now guides clients toward solutions tailored to their lifestyle and comfort. She never forgets how it feels to take the first courageous step.

"You are already beautiful; we are here to reveal it together." — Sheri Blow

Faith in action

A lifelong believer, Sheri weaves gentle affirmations and scripture into her work when clients welcome it. From whispered prayers before fittings to handwritten notes with each delivery, she honors the sacredness of your story.
