# Island Style Wigs Roadmap

> Last updated: 2025-11-04

## Phase 1 — MVP Storefront
- Polish core marketing pages (Home, About, Expert Help, Guides, Videos, Contact).
- Launch curated Shop highlights with clear consultation CTAs.
- Integrate Shopify collections for live inventory once credentials are available.
- Instrument analytics baseline for visits, navigation depth, and consultation requests.

## Phase 2 — AI Try-On Beta
- Connect favorites and consultation funnel to authenticated customer profiles.
- Stand up upload + job orchestration services with moderation and retry support.
- Deploy the wig transfer microservice and tune latency for a <10s round trip.
- Collect qualitative beta feedback from support staff and early customers.

## Phase 3 — Funnel Metrics & Optimization
- Track conversion from favorites → try-on → consultation booking in analytics warehouse.
- Launch CRM certification resources and nurture automations tied to try-on engagement.
- A/B test hero messaging, CTA placements, and follow-up emails for booked consults.
- Feed production telemetry into shared dashboards for marketing + operations.
