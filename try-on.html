Interactive Demo

Upload a portrait and preview Island Style favorites instantly.

This in-browser demo keeps your photo on the page and swaps in saved wig favorites using layered artwork. Adjust the fit, compare styles, and download a composite—no external services required.

  • Works best with centered, front-facing photos.
  • All processing happens locally in your browser for quick experiments.
  • Need inspiration? Load the sample portrait to test the controls.

Workflow

  1. Upload or load the sample portrait.
  2. Choose a saved wig look from favorites.
  3. Fine-tune position, scale, then download.

Ready for production? Replace the demo assets with catalog imagery or connect the page to your AI blending service.

    Tips

    Make the most of the demo

    • Swap the SVG overlays in assets/js/try-on-data.js with transparent PNGs of your wigs.
    • Match the portrait lighting to your catalog photography for natural blending.
    • When you are ready for AI, hook the selection events into your generation service.
